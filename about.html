About Us

Chandler's hair salon, began in 2009, by our owner and salon manager Chandler Tyrrell.

The salon was created for everyone and anyone to use. Regardless of requests, hair type or hair needs. We have an outstanding international team that are here, waiting and ready to cater to all your hair needs.

We also belive in supporting the environment and our local heritage of Ireland. By using natural and locally sourced hair product. We are able to achieve this goal, as well as your hair demands.

We open 6 days a week, and able to open upon advance booking and requests to cater for your special events (Weddings, Birthdays, Hen Do's, etc)
