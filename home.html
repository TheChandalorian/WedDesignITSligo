Welcome to Chandler's Salon,

placed in the very heart of the beautiful town of Sligo.

Our expertly educated team are here to deliver superior services with excellent customer service with a personal touch.

Whether it's a complete transformation, gradually change and evolve, or subtly maintanence we will be attentive and carefully listen to you and your requests, using our expertise to diagnose specific and required hair therapy.

We will then recommend a tailored solution to create a look that is characteristically yours. You will receive an initial full personal consultation, honest advice, resulting in the highest standard of treatment.

Our beautifully designed, warm and welcoming salon offers you a space to relax, indulge and unwind. You will find our team knowledgeable, but most importantly respectful and inclusive; each member handpicked and trained to deliver the finest treatments.
